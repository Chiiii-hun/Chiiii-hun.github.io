Career Paths

I think one career path the skills from my project supports is game development. The ability to create a game needs patience and resilience and I think people who have these qualities and are interested in debugging and coding is suitable for this career.
Another career that is related loosely is game design. To create a interesting and interactive game and pretty character needs artistic skill and knowledge of coding, I think people who have such qualities are suitable for this career.
